About Canyon

We are a team of investors, operators, and engineers with experience across Silicon Valley and Asia. We bring an owner-operator approach to every investment, partnering over a long-term horizon and actively deploying our broad network to create lasting value.

Invest

We invest in enduring technology companies globally. We have the flexibility and long-term mindset to partner at any stage of a company’s lifecycle. We provide capital, hands-on operational support, and access to our extensive network to drive value creation and accelerate growth alongside founders and management teams.

Acquire & Transform

We acquire software and technology-enabled businesses to transform and optimize them for long-term ownership. We restructure these companies by combining talent, technology, and data, with AI as the core driver of change, to drive faster innovation and stronger performance. This approach has produced significant revenue growth, cost reductions, and earnings expansion across our portfolio. Our companies serve enterprise and government clients globally.

Build

We selectively partner with exceptional founders to build transformative AI companies from breakthrough ideas into enduring global leaders that address meaningful market gaps. We take concentrated positions, embed deeply in the business, align ownership with contribution, and stay actively involved until each company is built to last.
