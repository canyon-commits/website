Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information in two ways: information you voluntarily provide to us and information we collect automatically through your use of our website.

Information You Provide to Us: When you contact us by sending an email to one of the addresses listed on our website, we collect the personal information you choose to provide. This typically includes your name, email address, and any information contained in your message or attachments.

Information We Collect Automatically: When you visit our website, we use third-party analytics services, specifically Google Analytics, to collect information about your visit. This helps us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. The information we collect automatically may include your device and browser type, website usage data (such as pages visited and time spent on the site), and general geographic location derived from your anonymized IP address. We have configured our analytics to process this information in a way that does not identify you personally.

HOW AND WHY WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for specific business purposes, as described below.

To Respond to Your Inquiries: We use the information you provide in your emails to respond to your specific requests or answer a media inquiry.

To Analyze and Improve Our Website: We use the automatically collected information to understand usage trends, administer the site, improve its functionality and user experience, and ensure its security.

To Comply with Legal Obligations: We may use your information as necessary to comply with applicable legal or regulatory requirements.

We are committed to the principle of purpose limitation. We will not use the personal information you provide for any purpose other than the one for which it was provided without your consent.

HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information for monetary consideration. We may, however, share your information with the following categories of third parties for the business purposes described above:

Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as website hosting and IT support.

Analytics Partners: We share the automatically collected website usage data with our analytics partner, Google LLC, to help us analyze and improve our site. As described in more detail in this policy, this activity may be considered "sharing" under certain state privacy laws.

Legal and Compliance Disclosures: We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law, in response to a subpoena or court order, or in connection with a lawful request from a government or regulatory authority.

DATA SECURITY

We have implemented and maintained reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the personal information we collect from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

YOUR DATA RIGHTS

Depending on your state of residence, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information.

We do not sell your personal information for monetary consideration. However, our use of third-party website analytics may be considered "sharing" under certain state privacy laws. To submit a request to access or delete your information, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Information" section. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law.

Please note that we may be unable to delete information that is necessary for us to comply with our legal or regulatory obligations, to complete a transaction for which the information was collected, or for other exceptions permitted by law.

ANALYTICS AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

Our website uses Google Analytics, which uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about how our website is used. The information generated about your use of the website is transmitted to Google. This information is used to compile statistical reports on website activity for us.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective when we post the revised policy on this website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will indicate when it was last revised.
