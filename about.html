Canyon is a global technology investor across buyouts, growth equity, and venture capital

We invest in enduring technology companies globally. We have the flexibility and long-term thinking to partner with companies at any stage of their lifecycle. We provide capital, hands-on operational support, and access to our extensive network across geographies and industries to drive value creation and accelerate growth alongside founders and management teams.

We acquire software and technology-enabled businesses and focus on transforming and optimizing them for long-term ownership. We restructure these companies by leveraging talent, technology, and data, with AI as a central driver of transformation to accelerate innovation and performance. This approach has delivered significant revenue growth, cost reductions, and earnings expansion across our portfolio. Our companies serve enterprise and government clients around the world.

We selectively partner with exceptional founders to build transformative AI companies from breakthrough ideas into enduring global leaders that address meaningful market gaps. When we build companies, we take concentrated positions, embed deeply in the businesses, align ownership with contribution, and maintain active operational involvement until they are built to last.

We are a group of investors, operators, and engineers with deep global experience across Silicon Valley and Asia, committed to partnering with founders building disruptive and transformative companies.
